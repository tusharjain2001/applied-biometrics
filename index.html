BioFi

Thermoplastic (PP and PE) combining agricultural waste with standard, recycled, or biobased polymers. Can be injection molded or extruded using industry standard equipment.

Cost Competitive

Enables manufacturers to cost effectively reduce their carbon footprint and dependence on virgin polymers.

Application Areas

Can be used across the household, automotive, appliance, electronics, and outdoor equipment industries.

BTR Board

Biothermoset resin (BTR) composite which competes favorably with materials like fiberglass and sheet metal on price and functionality.

Stable Inputs

Composed of up to 50% sustainable plant biomass which reduces cost variability.

Durable and Functional

Lasts for many years and is easy to maintain. Ideal for humid environments where metal would rust. Water, heat, and fire resistant.

Technology

Leveraging Nature

We use proprietary methods to enable high-strength adhesion between plant-based fiber and common resins like polypropylene (PP) or polyethylene (PE).
